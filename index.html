HOW IT WORKS

One visit to understand. A plan to act on.

Most patients move through these four steps before starting treatment — whether for a hearing concern or a speech and language one.

STEP 01

Consultation

We talk through your concern, history and goals — in person or on a call.

STEP 02

Assessment

Diagnostic testing (audiometric or speech-language) to measure exactly what's happening.

STEP 03

Plan

A treatment or therapy plan built around the results, explained in plain terms.

STEP 04

Follow-up

Fitting adjustments or therapy sessions, reviewed and refined over time.
